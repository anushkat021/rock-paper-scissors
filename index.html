Rock Paper Scissors

To play Rock, Paper, Scissors against the computer, select rock (fist), paper (open hand), or scissors (V with fingers).You can also use the keyboard buttons 'r','p' & 's' for selection of rock,paper and scissors respectively. The computer will make its choice. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock. Make your move, and let's see if you can outsmart the computer!

The scores will appear here.

You can reset the score using the reset button.
